Company Profile

Zhejiang Huacheng Packaging Materials Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the research, development, and production of new flexible packaging materials. Located in the Jinyi New District of Jinhua City, the company operates a modern manufacturing facility covering 38,000 square meters, with a total investment of 120 million RMB. Huacheng currently employs 85 staff members, including 15 professional technical personnel. Backed by strong technical capabilities and advanced production processes, the company primarily manufactures retortable CPP film, retort films, low-temperature films, tissue packaging films, matte films, laminated bag films, inflatable films, and metallized base films. The company is equipped with four state-of-the-art multi-layer co-extrusion cast film production lines, along with a full set of supporting equipment. Huacheng also maintains a comprehensive quality management system and a dedicated R&D team. Adhering to a customer-centric service philosophy and holding independent export rights, the company continues to invest in cutting-edge technology and equipment. With ongoing innovation and a focus on high-end product development, Huacheng is steadily strengthening its position in the CPP film industry, committed to delivering premium products and services to its clients.
